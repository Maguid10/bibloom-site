Frequently asked questions

Is Bibloom free?

Yes. You can read daily Bible verses for free. Bibloom Premium unlocks unlimited verses, the listening voice, all backgrounds, app icons and themes, with a 7 day free trial. Cancel anytime.

What is Bibloom?

Bibloom is a daily Bible verse app for iPhone. Swipe through powerful verses, listen in a calming voice, read the meaning behind each passage, and lay down your worries to receive a verse that speaks to you.

Which Bible translation do you use?

Bibloom uses the World English Bible (WEB), which is in the public domain.

Does Bibloom have verses for anxiety and stress?

Yes. Verses are organized by theme, including anxiety, fear, strength, peace, hope, rest and grace, so you can find the right words for how you feel.

Is Bibloom on Android?

Bibloom is on iPhone today, with Android coming soon.
